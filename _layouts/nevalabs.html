Msc Thesis

Can persuasive design patterns found in fitness applications encourage sign up to news applications?

I seek to understand why users don't want to sign up for news application and to explore how might design be used to persuade them otherwise. To do this, first I need understand what prevents people from carrying this out action and then try change this behaviour through a series of user experiments.
