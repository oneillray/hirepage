How I like to Work

  • I like to show designs and get feedback quickly and rather than do 'big reveals' so I would be sending stuff every week or even every couple of days.
  • I will create the initial UI design mockups using Photoshop CC (unless otherwise stated), I can provide these as PSDs well as the scratch files at the end of the project.
  • I work on the designs locally and upload the PSDs to a dropbox folder which I can share with you at the end.
  • For design feedback, I would like to use a tool such as invisionapp rather than sending JPGs over email. Alternatively we can do Skype meetings and review my work that way.
  • Communication wise, I like to services such as Slack and trello over email.
